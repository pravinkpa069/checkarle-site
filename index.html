    FAQ

    What are the default daily costs?

    Base per-person per-day (India): Backpacker ₹2,500, Comfort ₹5,000, Luxury ₹12,000. Regions multiply these bases.

    How do region multipliers work?

    SE Asia ×1.2, Europe ×2.0, Americas ×2.2, Middle East ×1.6, Africa ×1.5, Oceania ×2.4.

    What is included?

    Stay, food, local transport, activities buffer, flights (if provided), and contingency.
